Service Description
Haircut
  • Adult Cut: Looking for a quick haircut? This is the one for you.
  • Cut & Shampoo: This cut includes a shampoo service with some of our favorite professional products.
  • Cut, Shampoo & Basic Style: After this blow-dry and brush-finished cut, you'll be ready for anything.
  • Kids Cut: Help your little one stay looking great. For guests 12 and under. (Age may vary by location.)
  • Kids Cut & Shampoo: Add a fresh shampoo service to their fresh new haircut. For guests 12 and under.
Coloring
  • Permanent: Receive a professional hair color service performed by one of our experts to cover gray or to lighten or darken your natural hair color.
  • Semi: Looking for a new color, but not ready for a total commitment? Ask your stylist if semi-permanent is right for you.
  • Specialty: Make a bold change with an on-trend color choice, including dimensional color, multiple colors, ombre/sombre, balayage, block color, color melt, and more.
Beard
  • Cuts & Fades: Obviously, cuts and fades are a barber’s bread and butter, so it’s crucial that you’ve got these services on lock.
  • Straight-Razor Shaves: Straight-razor shaves are classic, iconic, and in-demand. A man can’t walk into just any old shop and expect to get a top-of-the-line straight razor shave.
  • Beard & Mustache Trims & Styles: A next-level barbershop experience should be about more than just the hair on your clients’ heads.
